Shree Devkikrishna Ravalnath Saunsthan

Shree Devkikrishna Ravalnath Saunsthan is one of the earliest temples set up by the Saraswat community in Goa brought to by the legendary Lord Parshuram.

The story of Devki and Krishna can be traced to the Mahabharata, a special reference to Gomanchal Parvat where it is reported that a battle was fought between Krishna and Jarasandha.
